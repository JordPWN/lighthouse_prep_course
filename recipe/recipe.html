Jordan's Toast

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter (You may substitute margarine)
  • Toaster
  • (optional) Knife to apply butter (finger will suffice if you cannot obtain a knife)

    • Directions

    1. Remove bread from bread bag. If it is not sliced, I'm sorry, I don't know how to proceed. I recommend asking for assistance from an adult.
    2. Place sliced bread in toaster.
    3. Press the toaster button. (See image 3)
    4. Wait.
    5. Apply butter to bread that is now toast. You are now ready to eat toast.
